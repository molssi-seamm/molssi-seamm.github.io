SEAMM Developers#

SEAMM is a free open-source software that is mainly developed by the team of Software Scientists at the Molecular Sciences Software Institute (MolSSI). As it is built for boosting productivity and scientific discovery in all domains of computational molecular sciences (CMS), we welcome any feedback and contributions from the members of the CMS community in both academia and industry.
